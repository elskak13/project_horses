Haflinger

Description

An intelligent and beautiful breed :)

Traits:

  • isabella/chestnut coat
  • light mane and tail
  • strong body
  • high intelligence
  • stubbornness

How to work with these horses:

  1. Get them from the pasture
  2. Brush them
  3. Clean their hooves
  4. Feed them snacks
  5. Release back into the pasture
